Designed for the Whole Team

CutBook bridges the gap between salon owners and stylists with transparent ledgers and friction-free tracking.

For Salon Owners

  • Real-time Revenue Tracking

    Monitor daily, weekly, and monthly salon income by payment methods like Cash, bKash, and Nagad.

  • Stylist Permissions & Approvals

    Control user roles and delegate work entry privileges. Approve or flag stylist logged entries directly.

  • Custom Service Catalog

    Set up standard haircut prices, shave rates, and custom packages with default commission shares.

For Stylists & Employees

  • Instant Work Entry Logging

    Stylists log cuts directly from their phones with auto-filled default service rates, reducing data entry.

  • Commission & Tip Calculations

    Automated breakdown computations of stylist earnings based on salon percentage rates and tips.

  • Accept & Track Payouts

    Stylists receive real-time notifications for daily payouts and approve or reject entries in their personal ledger.

Our Story & Vision

From an observation on the shop floor to a vision for Bangladesh's service economy.

"A few months ago, while working at Akij iBos, I noticed something that seemed small but kept bothering me. Many salon owners were still managing their entire business with notebooks, paper sheets, and manual calculations."

The Manual Struggle
  • ❌ Employee commissions on paper
  • ❌ Daily earnings tracked in booklets
  • ❌ Calculations done manually at closing
The CutBook Solution
  • ✅ Automated split-commission ledgers
  • ✅ Live synchronization & digital history
  • ✅ Instant, transparent payouts in one tap

I didn't start with a dream of building a billion-dollar company. I started with a simple question: "What if managing a salon could be as easy as using a smartphone?"

Since then, I've spent countless hours designing screens, writing code, fixing bugs, and rebuilding features. There were days when everything worked, and days when nothing worked. Days when I faced career setbacks, doubted myself, and wondered if anyone would even use this product. But every time I thought about giving up, I remembered the salon owners who still spent hours calculating commissions manually on paper. That kept me going.

Today, CutBook is becoming more than just an app. It's a management platform designed to help business owners track revenue, employee performance, commissions, payouts, and daily operations from a single place.

"While CutBook started with salons in mind, I believe the same problems exist across many service-based businesses—from barbershops and beauty salons to tailor shops and other commission-based workplaces. Our vision is to help thousands of businesses across Bangladesh move away from paper-based management and run operations efficiently with technology that is affordable, simple, and built around their real needs."

JR
Jaohar Raihan Founder, CutBook
