Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CutBook. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the operational data of your business (such as salons and tailors), employees, and clients. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, process, and safeguard the information you trust us with when utilizing the CutBook App and website.

2. Data Collection & Offline-First Design

CutBook is built as an offline-first system to ensure your business remains active at all times. All transactions, commission ledgers, and payout logs are saved locally in a secure SQLite database stored on your device.

When an active network connection is detected, CutBook automatically synchronizes your local data with our secure servers. This includes:

  • Employee profiles (names, custom commissions, and roles).
  • Operational logs (service item price tags, logs of services, stitch orders, haircuts, payments, and tips).
  • Owner metrics and historical logs to facilitate multi-device updates and secure backups.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information processed by CutBook is used strictly to provide the core system functionality:

  • Real-time business operational analytics and performance reporting.
  • Accurate commission calculations and payout request records for staff.
  • Synchronizing device ledgers across multiple authorized user logins.
  • Sending transactional, synchronization, or upcoming service notifications.

4. Data Security

We deploy standard industry practices to ensure your data is safe. Connection pipelines between local devices and our sync servers use secure Transport Layer Security (TLS) and WebSocket connections. Databases hosted on our production cloud servers utilize enterprise-grade encryption standard practices to protect your data from unauthorized access or breaches.

5. Third-Party Integrations

CutBook does not share or sell business ledgers or customer transactional history to advertising companies. We may use verified services (such as Google Analytics for mobile crash reports, Firebase, or similar) strictly to track performance improvements, app latency, and offline sync bugs.

6. Your Control Over Data

You have full authority over your data. As a business owner, you can edit, clear, or request absolute deletion of your synchronized business record profiles and accounts directly from the setting screen of the application or by writing to support.

7. Contact Information

If you have any inquiries, suggestions, or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or need help with database management, please reach out to us at:

Email: support@cutbook.app
